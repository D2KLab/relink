Automobile sales in the United States down sharply
February 03 , 2009
Sales of automobiles in the United States fell as much as 35 % in January , compared to the same month a year ago , as manufacturers reported their January sales .
Ford Motor Company sales dropped 42 % last month from the previous year .
General Motors reported a 49 % decline .
Chrysler was hardest hit among the domestically owned manufacturers with a plunge of 55 % .
The decline in sales has not been limited to just US manufacturers .
Japanese automaker Toyota reported a 32 % drop in US sales from a year earlier , Nissan Motors dropped 30 % , and Honda sales dropped 28 % .
Subaru and South Korean Hyundai Motor Company were two of the few auto firms that reported an increase in sales .
They posted gains of eight and fourteen percent , respectively .
