Apple releases iPhone SDK , announces upcoming update
March 06 , 2008
During a media event held at its Cupertino headquarters , Apple released the long awaited iPhones Software Development Kit ( SDK ) and the associated iTunes `` App Store '' and iPhone Developer Program .
The SDK will allow third party software developers to create applications for the iPhone and then distribute them on the iTunes Apps Stores .
Any members of the iPhone Developer Program can add applications to the store for free ; the program costs $ 99 ( USD ) to join .
Developers will be able to set the price of the applications or release them for free .
All applications will have to be approved by Apple before being allowed on the store .
Some of the disallowed categories are `` porn '' , excessive bandwidth users and anything malicious or illegal .
VoIP applications would be allowed over Wi-Fi only .
Enterprises can setup private pages on the store to distribute internal applications .
