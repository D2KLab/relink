Global markets plunge
October 10 , 2008
Stock markets across the world have fallen sharply with several seeing the biggest drop in their history .
Asian markets saw the biggest sell-off .
The Nikkei dropped 9.62 % to reach a 20 year low .
Japan also saw a collapse of a mid-size insurance company , Yamato Life Insurance Company , which declared bankruptcy .
The Hang Seng , which was one of the few markets that was positive yesterday , fell 7.19 % .
Australia dropped by 8.4 % and South Korea saw a 9 % fall .
In Europe , markets dropped at the open with the FTSE losing 11 % .
They have recovered only sightly with all European markets losing more than 5 % .
