US stocks log gains for fourth week in a row
April 04 , 2009
United States stock indexes posted gains on Friday , marking a four-week long positive streak .
This comes despite a report released by the US Labor Department on the same day , which said that the US economy shed 663,000 jobs in March , with the unemployment rate reaching 8.5 % , the highest since 1983 .
The Dow Jones Industrial Average posted a modest gain of half a percent or 39 points , ending the day at 8,017 .
The index has not closed above 8,000 since February .
The last time the Dow had risen for four weeks in a row was between September and October of 2007 , when the stock index reached its all-time high of over 14,000 .
The streak is the index 's best since 1993 .
The broader S&P 500 gained eight points , or one percent , by the closing bell .
The Nasdaq Composite had the largest gains of the three indexes , up 1.2 % to 1,622 .
