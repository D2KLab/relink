Dow Jones Industrial Average closes at lowest level in six years
February 20 , 2009
The Dow Jones Industrial Average closed at its lowest level in over six years on Thursday , having lost 1.16 % , or 89.68 points , bringing it to a level of 7,465.95 .
It had , at one point , set an intraday bear market low of 7,447.55 .
It was the lowest ending for the index since October 9 , 2002 .
The Dow has fallen almost fifteen percent since the start of this year .
Two other US indexes also fell today : the Standard & Poor 's 500 ( S&P ) fell by 9.48 points , or 1.20 percent , to 778.94 points .
The Nasdaq Composite lost 1.71 % or 25.15 points , closing at 1,442.82 .
The S&P had seen its fourth consecutive loss on Thursday , its longest losing streak since last October .
It has lost almost fourteen percent since the start of 2009 .
